The Best Movies According to Yaseen

My Favorite Sci-Fi Film

2001: A Space Odyssey

Tells the journey of man on Earth. Made in the 1960s, this timeless classic uses all sorts of movie magic techniques that make you feel like you are on an actual space voyage

My Favorite Action Film

The Raid: Redemption

Nonstop action from start to finish. A revolution in Martial Arts film making

My Favorite 90s Film

Jumanji

I would consider this as a borderline horror film because of the way it was directed and Robin Williams is incredible in this film
